Welcome to Old Lisbon Restaurants

PORTUGUESE CUISINE

“Old Lisbon opened its doors in January 1991 with one small location in Coral Way, Miami. Our second location opened in the city of South Miami in 2010 with a modern contrast. In September 2011, the location in Coral Way suffered a devastating fire, which destroyed most of its premises. The restaurant reopened in October 2013, with the same open kitchen and amazing dishes for which Old Lisbon is known. This time, completely remodeled and triple its original size, this location offers a friendlier family oriented atmosphere, in order to give its customers a true Portuguese gastronomic experience, the restaurant is complemented with a new Market & Bakery adjacent to the main room. Now, on the 25th anniversary, Old Lisbon opened its newest location in the city of Sunny Isles. Lastly, Old Lisbon is investing significantly in its catering services by hiring qualified people, equipping itself with top-of-the line appliances, and developing various menu options to better accommodate different possible venues, private and corporate. We look forward to seeing you soon at one of our locations or serving you at your next event.”
